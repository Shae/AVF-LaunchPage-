1. What are the 'top selling' app categories in the U.S.?

It appears that the most popular Apps downloaded in the US for 2011 were ranked in this order:

1)Games, 2)Weather, 3)Social 4)Maps/Nav/Search, 5)Music

But more importantly, Apps that downloaders are most willing to purchase were ranked in this order: 1)Games, 2)Entertainment, 3)Productivity, 4)Maps/Nav/Search, 5)Food, 6)And then News
